## About Atlas MK-II

Our second-generation competition robot features a redesigned intake system and improved climbing mechanism.

## Design Highlights

- **Intake**: Omni-wheel intake with active susCam
- **Elevator**: 4-stage lift reaching 30"
- **Climb**: Single-bar hang under 5 seconds
- **Drive**: 6-motor tank drive with rubberized wheels

This robot competed at the State Championship and helped us secure 2nd place overall.
